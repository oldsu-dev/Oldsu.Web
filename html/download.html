Download

Here are the instructions on downloading the client and what to do exactly. Probably something with a link to osekai for the clean client and the patched executable with a separate download and to replace the file(s) where needed.

System Requirements

Windows

- Anything from Windows 7 SP1 onwards (but preferably just use an OS that's still officially supported)

- Installed .NET 4.5

- A graphics card that is at least DX9 compatible and has OpenGL 1.1+ support


Linux

While we don't offer official support for any Linux distributions we still provide an install script and some help on Discord or in the forums when needed. Further information can be found in the linked repository or by just shooting us a message.
