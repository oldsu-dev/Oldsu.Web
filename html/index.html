What is oldsu?

oldsu! is a project that started in october 2020 by Eeveelution, the former owner of the initial project. The goal of it is, to provide a fully working experience in older versions of osu! currently including the 2008 client and a planned 2009 version (tbd). The current ownership lies by marshallracer, and is maintained by a volunteering team, to provide you the expierence many missed out on. oldsu! is trying to fix the flaws of 2008, we will never change gameplay in any way.
